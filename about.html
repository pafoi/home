About Plant a Forest of Ideas.

PaFoI is a gentle co‑creation space for people and organisations who want to explore real questions together and see which ideas might grow into projects, hubs, or ventures.

Why PaFoI exists.

Many conversations about change stop at inspiration. People leave with full notebooks but not always with shared, practical next steps. PaFoI was created to bridge that gap.

  • Create calm spaces where different voices can meet as equals.
  • Turn scattered insights into shared maps, sketches, and ideas.
  • Help promising ideas find homes in communities, programmes, or ventures.
  • Strengthen ecosystems by connecting people, not just projects.

How PaFoI works in practice.

PaFoI is not a single workshop or event. It is a way of moving: listening together, making together, and then tending what appears. Each collaboration is shaped with the people and places involved.

Shared stewardship

Ideas, relationships, and knowledge are treated as shared responsibilities. No single actor “owns” the whole forest.

Respect for context

We start from real places, communities, and histories — not just abstract decks or models.

From insight to action

We look for small, concrete next steps instead of only collecting inspiration or producing reports.

Learning in the open

Where it is safe to do so, we share patterns and tools so others can adapt them in their own contexts.

Who holds PaFoI.

PaFoI draws on years of ecosystem‑building, research, and venture‑building work from different practitioners and organisations. In this phase, it is supported by collaborators such as SCII and Think.Shift.Design, who contribute experience, networks, and infrastructure.

PaFoI itself is held as a neutral shared space. It does not exist to promote any single organisation, but to be a forest clearing where many actors can plant and grow ideas together.
