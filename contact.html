Contact PaFoI.

If something in this forest resonates with you, the easiest next step is a short note. You don’t have to have everything figured out before you reach out.

A good first email

What to include.

To help us respond meaningfully, you can share a little about yourself and what you’re holding. A few simple lines are enough.

  • Who you are and where you’re writing from.
  • A theme, question, or idea that’s on your mind.
  • Any community, group, or organisation connected to it.
  • What drew you to PaFoI or this forest of ideas.

Email: hello@pafoi.org

If you’re a partner

Exploring a collaboration.

If you represent an organisation and would like to explore a deeper collaboration, you can include a short overview of your context and what you’re curious about.

  • A sentence about your organisation and role.
  • The place(s) or communities you work with.
  • The theme or challenge you’d like to explore with others.
  • Any timelines, constraints, or hopes we should be aware of.

From there, we can schedule a short call to see whether PaFoI is a good fit, and what a first step might look like.
