Ideas are treated as seeds.

Many ideas are dismissed as “unrealistic” before they have a chance to breathe. PaFoI exists for people who want to look again — to see ideas as seeds that might grow, given time, care, and the right conditions.

Not every seed becomes a tree. That’s part of the forest. Here, we experiment in the open, learn together, and pay attention to what takes root — in communities, organisations, and ecosystems.

What happens inside PaFoI.

A few simple patterns, held with care: we gather, we make sense, we imagine, and we try things together.

Inspire
Quiet talks, story circles, and gentle provocations that open up new ways of seeing our places and possibilities.
Co‑create
Labs and sprints where people map challenges, sketch ideas, and shape concepts into something shareable.
Support
Follow‑through: light structures, introductions, and pathways so promising ideas can find homes in projects or ventures.

Who this is for.

PaFoI is not a club. It is a forest clearing. People step in from very different paths.

Individuals

  • Community organisers, practitioners, and creatives.
  • People holding questions, half‑formed ideas, or quiet experiments.
  • Those who prefer circles and sketches over stages and spotlights.

Organisations

  • Community groups, NGOs, and public agencies.
  • Enterprises exploring themes with communities, not just markets.
  • Ecosystem builders and funders who value grounded, co‑created ideas.

How we treat ideas and people.

Co‑creation depends on trust. A few simple commitments guide how we host and how we use what emerges.

  • You keep what you bring in. Your existing work and IP remain yours.
  • Shared insights travel, sensitive details don’t. We may share high‑level patterns with attribution; confidential elements stay in the circle.
  • Time‑bound exploration. If an idea looks like it could grow into a project or venture, we agree a short period to explore it together and decide how to move.
  • People before projects. We pay attention to capacity, consent, and context. No one is pressured to scale beyond what feels right.

A fuller participation and IP note will be available for partners and contributors who need the details.
