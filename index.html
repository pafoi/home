About

We treat ideas like seeds.

Many ideas are dismissed as “unrealistic” before they have a chance to breathe. PaFoI exists for those ideas — and for the people who quietly care about them.

We make space to look again: to see ideas as seeds that might grow, given time, attention, and the right soil in communities and organisations.

Not every seed becomes a tree. That’s okay. What matters is tending the forest: learning, trying, and noticing what begins to take root.

Inside PaFoI

What actually happens here.

A few simple patterns, held with care. Nothing flashy — just enough structure so people can think, feel, and build together.

Listen

Gentle dialogues and story circles where people share what is real in their places, work, and lives.

Co‑create

Labs and sprints that turn scattered thoughts into shared sketches and ideas we can see on the table.

Tend

Light follow‑through: checking back in, making introductions, and exploring possible homes for promising ideas.

Who it's for

People who like circles more than stages.

PaFoI is for those who are more interested in shared work than personal spotlight.

Individuals

  • Community organisers, practitioners, and creatives.
  • People holding questions or half‑formed ideas.
  • Those who learn best by listening and making together.

Organisations

  • Community groups, NGOs, and public agencies.
  • Enterprises exploring themes with communities, not just markets.
  • Ecosystem builders and funders seeking grounded, co‑created ideas.

Participate

Ways to step into the forest.

  • Join a session. Spend a few hours in a dialogue or lab; listen, share, and see what resonates.
  • Bring a question. If you hold a challenge or theme, explore a co‑created process around it.
  • Support a project. Partner with initiatives that grow out of PaFoI as a host, ally, or funder.

Have a seed of an idea?

It doesn’t need a deck, a budget, or a five‑year plan. If it matters to you and to the people around you, it’s worth a conversation.
