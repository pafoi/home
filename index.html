Ideas are treated like seeds.

Many ideas are dismissed as “unrealistic” before they have a chance to breathe. PaFoI exists for those ideas — and for the people who quietly care about them.

We make space to look again: to see ideas as seeds that might grow, given time, attention, and the right soil in communities and organisations.

Not every seed becomes a tree. That’s okay. What matters is tending the forest: learning, trying, and noticing what begins to take root.

Learn more on the About page.

What happens inside PaFoI.

A few simple patterns, held with care. Nothing too fancy — just clear spaces to listen, think, and build together.

Listen

Gentle dialogues and story circles where people share what is real in their places and work.

Co‑create

Labs and sprints that turn scattered thoughts into shared sketches and ideas on the wall.

Tend

Light follow‑through: checking back in, making introductions, and exploring homes for ideas.

See more formats and examples on What we do.

Who this is for.

PaFoI is for people who like circles more than stages, and for organisations who want to work with communities, not just talk about them.

Individuals

  • Community organisers, practitioners, and creatives.
  • People holding questions or half‑formed ideas.
  • Those who enjoy learning and making with others.

Organisations

  • Community groups, NGOs, and public agencies.
  • Enterprises exploring themes with communities, not just markets.
  • Ecosystem builders and funders seeking grounded, co‑created ideas.
